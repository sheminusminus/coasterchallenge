About the event

What’s the cause / beneficiary of Coaster Challenge?

All of the money raised during the Coaster Challenge goes to Special Olympics Illinois. More information about the organization can be found on the Special Olympics Illinois website, here.

The Coaster Challenge takes place on September 25, 2016 from 9:00 am – 5:00 pm.

Where is Coaster Challenge 2016 taking place?

The Coaster Challenge is taking place at Six Flags Great America. The address is 1 Great America Parkway, Gurnee, IL 60031

Participants

I’m a coaster rider. What do I need to do in preparation for Coaster Challenge?

All participants will receive a packet that includes a tshirt and their ticket to the park. You can pick this packet up the day of the event, at Six Flags or at an earlier date at specific location. All participants will also be required to sign a waiver prior to the event. Free parking passes will only be given to individuals who pick up their packets at an earlier date.

  • September, 23 – 500 Waters Edge, Ste. 100, Lombard, IL, 10 am – 6 pm
  • September, 23 – Gurnee location TBA, 10 am – 1 pm

Can I pick up someone else’s packet?

If you are picking up a packet for another individual, you must bring the original signed waiver along with their offline donations.

Do participants get free food?

Yes! All participants will be given breakfast. Those who fundraise at the 10k level and above will also be given lunch vouchers.

Getting there

Is there free parking for participants?

Yes! All coaster riders receive a free parking pass. Free parking passes will only be given to individuals who pick up their packets ahead of time though, so please plan accordingly!

Packet Pick-up Dates:
  • September, 23 – 500 Waters Edge, Ste. 100, Lombard, IL, 10 am – 6 pm
  • September, 23 – Gurnee location TBA, 10 am – 1 pm

Can I take public transportation to the event?

Pace Suburban Bus provides direct service from Schaumburg and from Rosemont to Six Flags Great America on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays from June to Labor Day. The schedule may vary depending on park hours. For fare and schedule information, please call (847) 228-2416 or get more information here.

For train schedules, please visit Metra’s website or call (312) 322-6777 between 8 a.m. and 5 p.m. CST, or (312) 836-7000 after 5 p.m. CST.

Who is Donosaur?
